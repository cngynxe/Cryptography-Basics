Types of Ciphers

Type Description Examples
Transposition Cipher Changes the position of the character, but leaves the identity of the character unchanged. Railfence; Columnar Transpoition; other route ciphers
Monoalphabetic Substitution Cipher Each letter is replaced with another. Polybius Square; Baconian Cipher; Caesar Cipher
Polyalphabetic Ciphers Diffeerent alphabets are used to encipher letters depending on their position. Porta Cipher; Vigenere Cipher; Autokey
Polygraphic Substitution Groups of characters are replaced. Hill Cipher; Playfair; Foursquare
